Frequently asked

What happens after I pay?
Your payment is processed instantly by Razorpay. You will receive an email confirmation. Within minutes, your API key tier in the dashboard will be upgraded. If it takes longer than 30 minutes, email us at hello@gstaccelerator.in.
Is this a subscription or one-time payment?
Each plan is a single payment for 30 days of access — not a subscription. There are no recurring charges. You simply renew when your access period ends, only if you choose to.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major Indian payment methods are accepted via Razorpay: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking (all major banks), and wallets.
Do I get a GST invoice?
Yes. A GST-compliant tax invoice is emailed to you automatically after payment for all paid plans. If you need it on your company GSTIN, add your GST number in the Razorpay checkout form.
Can I try before I buy?
Absolutely. The Free tier gives you 100 API calls/month with no credit card required. Sign up at /dashboard and get your key immediately.
Why use an API instead of manual lookup?
Automating GST lookups saves hundreds of hours and reduces compliance errors. Read our detailed comparison: GST API vs Manual Lookup.
